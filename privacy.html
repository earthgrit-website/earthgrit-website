Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

EarthGrit ("we", "us", "our") is a handmade food brand based in Milton Keynes, England, United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. What Information We Collect

    >Your name — to identify your order >Your email address — to send you an order confirmation >Your delivery address and phone number — for order fulfilment only >Your payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details

2. How We Use Your Information

    >To process and fulfil your order >To send you a single order confirmation email after purchase >To deliver your order to the correct address

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, newsletters, or any other purpose.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

When you place an order, your details (name, email, delivery address, phone number, and order summary) are securely stored in Google Firebase (Firestore Database), hosted on Google Cloud servers within the EU/UK region.

This data is used solely to process your order. It is not shared, sold, or used for any other purpose. Firebase is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

Google's privacy policy applies to this storage: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Payment Security

All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment provider. When you pay, your card details go directly to Stripe's secure servers. We never see or store your card number.

Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/gb/privacy

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

    >Stripe — to securely process your payment >Google Firebase — to securely store your order details >Delivery services — only your name and address, where required to fulfil your order >Where required by UK law

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your order data for up to 12 months for order fulfilment and legal record-keeping purposes. After this period, your data is deleted from our systems.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

    >Access the personal data we hold about you >Correct any inaccurate data >Request deletion of your data at any time >Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at earthgrit@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This tool collects anonymous data such as pages visited, time on site, and device type. It does not identify you personally.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

9. Cookies

This website uses:

    >Essential cookies — required for the shopping cart to function >Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, to help us understand site usage (only if you accept cookies)

You can decline cookies using the banner on our site and no analytics data will be collected.
